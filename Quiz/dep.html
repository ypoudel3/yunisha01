Depression Test

1. I feel down, depressed, or hopeless most of the time.

2. I have lost interest or pleasure in activities I used to enjoy.

3. I experience changes in my appetite (eating too much or too little).

4. I feel tired or have little energy most days.

5.I have thoughts that I would be better off dead, or of hurting myself

6. I have trouble sleeping or sleep too much.
