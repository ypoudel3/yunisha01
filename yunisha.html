Minders Mission

Minders is on a mission to empower peoples mental wellbeing through mental health education. We utilize evidence-based and data-driven methods to help people understand themselves and others in ways that are proven to enhance mental health and prevent mental illness. Minder’s overarching cause is to help develop and implement free of cost mental health education programs in underserved communities.
