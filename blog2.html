Negative Aspects Leading to Mental Health Issues

Comparison and Self-Esteem: The constant comparison on social media can negatively affect self-esteem and body image, leading to anxiety and depression.

Cyberbullying: The anonymity of social media can lead to cyberbullying, which has significant psychological consequences.

Addiction and Anxiety: The addictive nature of social media can contribute to anxiety, stress, and disturbed sleep patterns.

Tips for Healthy Life

  • Mindful Usage: Set time limits and curate a positive feed to use social media mindfully.
  • Digital Detox: Take regular breaks from social media to recharge mentally.
